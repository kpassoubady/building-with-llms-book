## Common Issues Identified:
1. **Formatting**: Knowledge Check answers missing the literal word "Answer" inside the `1. **Answer**:` prefix (affecting all chapters).
2. **Structure**: Heavy overuse of `[!TIP]` admonitions (affecting all chapters, exceeding the 1-2 limit).
3. **Structure**: Consecutive admonitions stacked without separating text (affecting most chapters).
4. **Structure**: Invalid custom admonition tags like `[!PROMPT]`.
5. **Prose**: LLM prose tells such as "Let's", "not just", and "worth noting" found scattered across chapters.
